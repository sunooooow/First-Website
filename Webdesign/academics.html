Academics

Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED)

This program is designed to prepare future educators for the foundational years of schooling. Students engage in comprehensive studies of child development, learning theories, and classroom management strategies tailored for learners from kindergarten through primary grades. The curriculum covers pedagogical methods for core subjects like mathematics, science, and language arts, alongside extensive practicum experiences in real elementary school settings. Graduates are equipped to foster critical thinking, literacy, and social skills in young children, becoming licensed professionals ready to shape the next generation.

Bachelor of Physical Education (BPED)

This degree focuses on the science of human movement, health, and athletic performance. Students learn to design and implement physical activity programs that promote lifelong wellness and fitness. The coursework includes exercise physiology, sports pedagogy, kinesiology, and the psychology of sport. Through practical training and coaching internships, students develop the skills to lead physical education classes, manage school sports teams, and organize community fitness initiatives. Graduates emerge as certified instructors capable of inspiring physical activity and healthy lifestyles in diverse educational and community environments.

Teacher Certificate Program (TCP)

academic and professional training course designed to cultivate deeply knowledgeable and skilled educators capable of shaping young minds. Unlike a general overview of requirements, a TCP is an immersive educational journey that immerses candidates in the science of learning, developmental psychology, and the art of pedagogy. The curriculum focuses on mastering instructional strategies, differentiated learning techniques, and inclusive classroom practices to ensure every student thrives. It emphasizes the theoretical foundations of education, encouraging candidates to become reflective practitioners who can critically analyze educational challenges and adapt their teaching methods to foster a dynamic and supportive learning environment. Ultimately, the program aims to produce teachers who are not just compliant with standards, but are inspiring leaders equipped to drive educational excellence and student success.
